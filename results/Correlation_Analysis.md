# 📈 Correlation Analysis

## 📖 Overview

Correlation analysis was conducted to investigate the relationships between key contractor characteristics, business performance indicators, and operational factors within the construction industry in Madurai.

The primary objective of this analysis was to determine how variables such as annual turnover, customer ratings, employee count, company age, specialization, and service offerings interact with one another. Understanding these relationships helps homeowners, developers, and researchers make informed decisions when evaluating contractors and assessing housing affordability.

The findings provide valuable insights into contractor performance patterns and reveal factors that contribute to business success and customer satisfaction.

---

# 🎯 Objectives of Correlation Analysis

The correlation analysis was designed to:

* Identify relationships between contractor performance indicators.
* Examine factors influencing annual turnover.
* Evaluate the impact of workforce size on business growth.
* Understand how customer ratings relate to contractor characteristics.
* Explore the relationship between company age and business performance.
* Support evidence-based contractor selection.

---

# 📊 Statistical Approach

Correlation analysis measures the strength and direction of relationships between variables.

For this study, correlation techniques were applied to examine associations among:

* Financial indicators
* Workforce characteristics
* Customer satisfaction metrics
* Business maturity indicators

The analysis helps identify whether increases or decreases in one variable are associated with changes in another.

# ⭐ Correlation Between Ratings and Annual Turnover

One of the key relationships examined was the association between customer ratings and annual turnover.

## Findings

The analysis revealed a generally positive relationship between:

* Higher customer ratings
* Increased annual turnover

Contractors receiving strong customer reviews often demonstrated:

* Greater market trust
* Higher project acquisition rates
* Better business growth

---

## Interpretation

Customer satisfaction plays an important role in business success.

Positive reviews and strong reputations contribute to:

* Increased referrals
* Greater client retention
* Enhanced financial performance

However, turnover is also influenced by additional factors such as project scale, workforce capacity, and business experience.

---

# 👷 Correlation Between Employee Count and Turnover

The relationship between workforce size and annual turnover was examined to understand organizational capacity.

## Findings

A moderate positive relationship was observed between:

* Number of Employees
* Annual Turnover

Larger organizations generally reported:

* Higher revenues
* Greater project handling capability
* Increased operational capacity

---

## Interpretation

Workforce size contributes to project execution capabilities and revenue generation.

However, several smaller firms also demonstrated strong turnover despite maintaining lean teams, indicating that efficiency and specialization can sometimes outweigh workforce size.

---

# 🏢 Company Age and Annual Turnover

Business maturity was analyzed to determine whether older companies generate higher revenues.

## Findings

The correlation between:

* Company Age
* Annual Turnover

was relatively weak.

Several newly established firms reported competitive turnover figures, while some older firms showed moderate revenue levels.

---

## Interpretation

Company age alone is not a reliable predictor of financial success.

Other factors such as:

* Service quality
* Market demand
* Project portfolio
* Customer relationships

appear to play a more significant role.

---

# ⭐ Company Age and Customer Ratings

The study also evaluated whether longer-established contractors receive higher ratings.

## Findings

Only a weak relationship was identified between:

* Years of Operation
* Customer Ratings

Both newer and older firms demonstrated high ratings.

---

## Interpretation

Customer satisfaction depends more on:

* Service quality
* Communication
* Project delivery
* Reliability

than on business age alone.

---

# 🛠️ Service Offerings and Ratings

The number and diversity of services offered by contractors were analyzed against customer ratings.

## Findings

A positive relationship was observed between:

* Service Diversity
* Customer Ratings

Contractors offering broader service portfolios generally received higher ratings.

---

## Common Service Categories

* Interior Design

## Interpretation

Customers often prefer contractors capable of handling multiple project requirements under a single service provider.
