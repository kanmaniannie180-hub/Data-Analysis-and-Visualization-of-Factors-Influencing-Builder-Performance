# 📍 Geographical Insights

## 📖 Overview

Geographical analysis plays an important role in understanding the distribution, accessibility, and performance of construction contractors across different regions of Madurai. Contractor location can significantly influence project costs, service availability, workforce accessibility, material procurement, and customer satisfaction.

This study examines the spatial distribution of contractors and identifies regional trends in contractor performance, business growth, customer ratings, and service specialization. By analyzing geographical patterns, homeowners and developers can make more informed decisions when selecting contractors for residential and commercial projects.

The analysis highlights key construction hubs, emerging development zones, and areas demonstrating strong contractor performance.

---

# 🎯 Objectives of Geographical Analysis

The geographical analysis was conducted to:

* Identify regions with a high concentration of contractors.
* Analyze location-based differences in contractor performance.
* Examine geographical patterns in customer ratings.
* Explore regional variations in business growth and turnover.
* Identify construction hotspots within Madurai.
* Support location-based contractor selection.

---
