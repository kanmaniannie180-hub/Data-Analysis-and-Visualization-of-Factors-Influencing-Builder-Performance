# 🔮 Future Work

## 📖 Overview

This study demonstrates how data analytics and Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) can support contractor evaluation and housing affordability assessment in Madurai. While the current research provides valuable insights into contractor performance, business characteristics, and construction trends, several opportunities exist to expand the scope and impact of the project.

Future work can leverage advanced analytics, machine learning, geographic information systems, and real-time market data to create a more comprehensive decision-support framework for homeowners, construction firms, investors, and policymakers.

The following sections outline potential directions for future research and development.

---

* Customer Reviews

### Possible Algorithms

* Decision Trees
* XGBoost
